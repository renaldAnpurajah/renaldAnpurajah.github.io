Accountability Tracking System

A short demonstration video

Technologies used: Sharepoint, Power Automate, Power Query, Forms and Excel

One major challenge that we faced in the programme accountability was that even though the project teams were implementing the accountability framework, often the tracking and reporting were not captured properly or not reported as per the requirements. I developed a digitized system using Microsoft 365 apps. The system was semi-automated. Further, I created the attached promotional video to share with our field teams and stakeholders to build up anticipation for it. The system is implemented successfully and it is to be scaled up to be used with sponsorship projects too.
